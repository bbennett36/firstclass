First Class Travelers LLC. Company Values Page

Customer Service:

We employ qualified personnel to deliver exceptional service to our customers. First Class Travelers Management team train all of its Crew members to ensure the best possible service to our customers.

Safety:

Safety is our main goal here at First Class Travelers. A thorough training program, along with retraining of our Drivers and Crew members ensures your safety. In addition, we do a rigorous drug, alcohol, and criminal background check on all employees. Our safety is not limited to only the highway, we train all crew members to deal with a wide variety of situations that may occur.

Integrity:

First Class Travelers employees are held to the highest level of standards, values, honesty, and integrity. We go to great lengths to train and retrain employees. We do not tolerate dishonesty and will dismiss any employees who are dishonest with the Company and or to our customers. We view all of our riders as a valued customer and focus on building a long-term relationships.
