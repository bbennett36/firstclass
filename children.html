Children Discount & Policy

Children discounted tickets are available for children ages 2 - 9 years old. Children may receive a 10% discount ticket. Tickets must be purchased at First Class Travelers office or at terminal agencies only. Discount tickets will not be available online.

Children discounted tickets can only be purchased together with an adult ticket. For every 1 adult ticket, only 2 children tickets can be purchased. If traveling with 3 or more children, two adult tickets would have to be purchased to obtain the child discount tickets. Only 2 children discounted tickets per 1 adult ticket. The child and adult must travel together on the same schedule, same time, and same day.

A child under 2 years of age does not require a ticket to travel but must be secured in the guardians lap for the duration of the trip (a child under the age of 2 cannot occupy a seat without being secured in a child safety seat). If a child safety seat will occupy a seat there will be a fare at childrenâ€™s discounted rate.

If traveling with 2, or more, children under the age of 2, the second (third etc.) Child passenger will require a children discount ticket, but must be secured in the guardians lap for the duration of the trip and the second child must be secured in a child safety seat.

Children 10 - 17 years of age may travel as an unaccompanied at full adult fare if the unaccompanied child guidelines are met in their entirety. There is no additional charges or fees for an unaccompanied minor. Please view the unaccompanied child guidelines. (HERE)

Please inform the Ticket Agent you would like the discount before any purchases are made. We are not able to assume a customer is eligible for a specific discount. Refunds are not provided for discounts tickets, or any difference in fare, once the sale is complete.

MILITARY DISCOUNT & POLICIES

First Class Travelers is proud to provide discounted transportation to those who serve our country. Active duty and Veterans may receive a (30%) discount off the standard fare.

The Military discounts applies to Active Duty, Veterans, National Guard, Reservists of the United States Armed Forces, which includes the Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines, and Coast Guard. A valid Military identification card must be presented upon request.

Military discounts are given at the First Class Travelers office or at terminal agencies only. Discount tickets will not be available online.

Please inform the Ticket Agent you would like the discount before any purchases are made. We are not able to assume a customer is eligible for any specific discount. Refunds are not provided for discounts, or any difference in fare once the sale is complete. A valid identification card must be presented upon request.

SENIOR DISCOUNT & POLICIES

Passengers age 60 and older may request a 10% discount on standard terminal fares. A valid photo ID is required.

Senior discount tickets are only available for purchase at terminals and agencies and are not available online.

The senior fare may not be combined with any other discounts including the advance, children, student, military, commuter or casino discounts.

Please inform the Ticket Agent you would like the senior discount before making any purchases. We are not able to assume a customer is eligible for any specific discount. Refunds are not provided for discounts, or any difference in fare once the sale is complete. A valid identification card must be presented upon request.

STUDENT DISCOUNT & POLICIES

Student may request a 10% discount on a standard fare ticket. To qualify you must be an active students currently enrolled in a college, university, community/junior college, etc.

The student discount may only be active when school is in session.

Student discounts are only available for purchase at terminals or agencies and are not available online.

Student fares may not be combined with any other discounts including the advance, children, Military, senior, or commuter.

Please inform the Ticket Agent you would like the discount before purchase, as we are not able to assume a customer is eligible for any specific discount. Refunds are not provided for discounts, or any difference in fare once the sale is complete. A valid identification card must be presented upon request.
