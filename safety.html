First Class Travelers LLC. Safety Page

The safety and security of our passengers is our top priority. Our excellent record of safe operation is a high standard we will always practice and live up to every day. Our buses operate in full compliance with — and in many cases exceed — all U.S. DOT safety regulations. The following are just a few examples of what we do to ensure a safe environment for our passengers and crew members, while maintaining our excellent record of safety:

  • All of First Class Travelers LLC Drivers and crew undergo comprehensive regular safety and emergency training that meets or exceeds all regulatory requirements.
  • Our Drivers and crew members undergo specific training to handle emergency situations. Crew roles, responsibilities and duties are clearly defined and assigned to handle almost any emergency on board.
  • The experience of our Drivers; All new Drivers Must have a minimum of 5 years prior over the road driving Commercial vehicle experience, with a CLEAN driving record to joining us. Plus pass the classroom and on the road test First Class Travelers provide.
  • All of First Class Travelers buses are designed and operated in compliance with strict requirements of the law, which ensure that they are able to remain safe under a variety of adverse conditions.
  • All of our buses are equipped with the most advanced technology, and our Drivers are expertly trained in its use.
  • First Class Travelers undergoes an inspection as mandated by the U.S. DOT, which verifies safety processes and procedures.
  • Our Drivers are also randomly drug tested without any prior notification.
